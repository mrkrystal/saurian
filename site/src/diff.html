About Saurian

In the game Star Fox Adventures, Krystal and the natives of Dinosaur Planet speak in a cipher langauge known as Dino Language. In later Star Fox games, Dinosaur Planet would come to be known as Sauria. The term Saurian was chosen as an unofficial term for the fictional language because it is easier to say and the planet's name changed. Watch this video to see an example of Saurian being spoken in the game.

Conversion Rules

According to the Star Fox Adventures Instruction Manual, page 28, these are the rules of converting between English and Saurian.

Vowels

The vowels are shifted 2 vowel positions forward when translating to Saurian. A E I O U - English U O A E I - Saurian

Letter M

The letter M does not change during translation. M - English M - Saurian

Letter Y

Saurian does not have a Y letter, so a zero (0) is used as a placeholder. Y - English 0 - Saurian

Remaining Letters

The remaining letters are shifted approximately 12 positions forward. B C D F G H J K L N P Q R S T V W X Z - English R S T V W X Z B C D F G H J K L N P Q - Saurian
